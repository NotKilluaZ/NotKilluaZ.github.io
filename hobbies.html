Hobbies

Badminton

My favourite sport at the moment is Badminton. I have been playing the sport since grade 3, although only casually for the longest time. It was only in grade 7 that I decided to take badminton more competitively by joining the school team. However, I had to stop playing completely once Covid-19 struck in grade 8. I started back up in grade 10, my first year of highschool when I excitingly made the school team again. I went on to make the team the next year, and started going to Clear One, a badminton facility to train even more during the school's off-season. My racket of choice right now is the Yonex Arc Saber 11, paired with a blue pair of Yonex Aerus 3's.

Ski & Snowboarding

Second to badminton, skiing and snowboarding is my next favourite sport. Having lived close to the Rocky Mountains my whole life, my family decided to buy a condo on the Panorama Mountain Resort. Since I was about 9 years old, I have been coming to this ski hill every winter and learned skiing and snowboarding my whole life. I completed all levels of lessons that the ski resort offered by the time I was 13 where I then joined the All Mountain Program. The All Mountain Program (AMP) was where I could improve my skills beyond the normal levels such as by learning avalanche safety, snow science, and most importantly, how to ride any slope I wanted! Chess

In the past year or two, I have enjoyed studying chess. I usually play online against friends, bots, or just complete mini puzzles. YouTube videos are also a great way I am able to improve my game as I can learn from very highly rated players such as GM Hikaru Nara. I find great pleasure in being able to outsmart your opponent being always being a step ahead and seeing different outcomes on the chess board. Although I do not take chess too seriously, I do want to keep playing and improving.
