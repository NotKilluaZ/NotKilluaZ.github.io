Enter The Abyss

You are an explorer who roams the Abyss in search of ancient treasure. You have heard rumors that the oldest, most valuable treasure is a sword hidden deep within the Abyss, guarded by many monsters and even Dragons! Being the curious explorer you are, you decide to seek out this treasure... While exploring the Abyss, you stumble across three mystical spirits who inhabit the abyss. These spirits each tell you to follow them at once. However, all of them are unique, and you cannot decide which one to pick. You realize that the rest of your treasure hunting journey is dependent completely on luck, and whether you choose to follow the correct spirit.
